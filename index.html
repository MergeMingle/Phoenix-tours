DESTINATIONS

Nature and Outdoor Attractions

Big Almaty Lake

Discover the tranquility of this high-altitude lake, surrounded by snow-capped mountains. Ideal for hiking and soaking in the natural beauty of Almaty’s alpine landscapes.

Charyn Canyon

Experience Kazakhstan’s very own “Grand Canyon.” Marvel at its stunning rock formations and explore the rugged terrain, perfect for adventurers and nature lovers.

Kolasay Lakes

Embark on a journey to the breathtaking Kolsay Lakes, a serene oasis tucked within the mountains. A perfect getaway for hiking, camping, and connecting with nature.

Kaindy Lakes

Visit the mysterious Kaindy Lake, where a submerged forest rises eerily from the turquoise waters. A one-of-a-kind natural wonder for photographers and explorers.

Turgen Waterfalls

Immerse yourself in the lush surroundings of the Turgen Gorge as you hike to the stunning waterfalls, a refreshing escape into nature.

Altyn Emel National Park

Venture into this unique national park, home to the famous “Singing Dune” and diverse wildlife, offering an unforgettable experience for eco-tourists and outdoor enthusiasts.

DESTINATIONS

Parks and Recreation

Panfilov Park

Step into history at Panfilov Park, a lush green space that houses monuments, serene walkways, and the iconic Ascension Cathedral.

First President's Park

Unwind in this vast urban park with its beautifully landscaped gardens and fountains, offering a perfect spot for relaxation and leisure activities.

Oi-Qaragai Mountain Resort

Explore this all-season mountain resort, offering skiing in winter and thrilling outdoor activities like hiking and mountain biking in the summer.

Kok Tobe Hill

Take a cable car to Kok Tobe Hill for sweeping panoramic views of Almaty. Enjoy the amusement park, restaurants, and cultural experiences at the summit.

Republic Square

A significant cultural hub in Almaty, Republic Square is the site of important public events and monuments, capturing the spirit of the city’s vibrant history.

Medeu Ice Skating Rink

Skate at the world’s highest-altitude ice rink, surrounded by stunning mountain vistas. It’s a must-visit for winter sports enthusiasts.

DESTINATIONS

Historical and Cultural Landmarks

Ascension Cathedral

Visit this architectural gem, a wooden Orthodox cathedral built without nails. Its vibrant colors and unique design make it one of Almaty’s most notable landmarks.

Almaty Central Mosque

Admire the beautiful blend of traditional Islamic and modern architecture at Almaty’s largest mosque, a spiritual and cultural center for the city.

Central State Museum of Kazakhstan

Explore Kazakhstan’s rich history at the Central State Museum, home to a vast collection of artifacts that span the country’s cultural and historical legacy.

Tamgaly Tas

Discover ancient petroglyphs at this fascinating historical site, where centuries-old rock carvings tell the story of Kazakhstan’s spiritual and cultural past.

DESTINATIONS

Shopping and Entertainment

Dostyk Plaza

Shop, dine, and unwind at Dostyk Plaza, a modern shopping destination featuring top local and international brands, as well as diverse entertainment options.

Esentai Mall

Indulge in luxury shopping at Esentai Mall, home to high-end international brands and fine dining, offering an upscale shopping experience.

Almaty Tower

Soar to new heights at Almaty Tower, where you can enjoy panoramic views of the city and its surrounding landscapes. A striking architectural icon.

Almaty Zoo

Spend a family-friendly day at Almaty Zoo, home to a variety of animals from around the world, offering an educational and entertaining experience for all ages.
